Do not use em dashes (—) in any content, copy, or UI text.

**Why:** User explicitly requested this. No reason given beyond preference.

**How to apply:** Replace em dashes with a period, comma, or reword the sentence. Examples:
- "specialist — not an algorithm" → "specialist, not an algorithm"
- "fundamentals — no coding background" → "fundamentals. No coding background"
- "works at SARS — not sourced" → "works at SARS. Not sourced"
